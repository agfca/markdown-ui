About

It [Markdown-UI] helps the developer to focus on the big picture on paper or text-editors, and with few details it lessen the cognitive load which means having meaningful productivity which equates to happiness! - Joel Bryan Juliano (author of Markdown-UI)

Markdown-UI is for

creating responsive UIs for mobile and web
easily communicating your UI
rapid prototyping
being more productive by having less cognitive load
UI/UX brain-storming sessions via email, whiteboard, text editor, paper, etc.
